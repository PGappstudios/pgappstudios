# Fasting and Walking: The iOS App That Tracks Intermittent Fasting & Daily Steps Together

Two of the most effective and accessible health habits — intermittent fasting and daily walking — finally have a dedicated app. **Fasting and Walking** is a free iOS app that lets you track both simultaneously, with clean visuals, science-based fasting stage information, and no unnecessary complexity.

No account required. No distractions. Just you, your fast, and your steps.

**Download free:** [Fasting and Walking on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/pt/app/fasting-and-walking/id6755205850?l=en-GB)

## What Is Fasting and Walking?

Fasting and Walking is a health and fitness app for iPhone that combines two of the most researched weight management approaches:

1. **Intermittent fasting** — timed periods of not eating that trigger metabolic benefits
2. **Daily walking** — consistent low-intensity movement proven to improve health markers

Most apps treat these separately. Fasting and Walking combines them in one clean interface, so you always see both your fasting progress and your step count at a glance.

## Fasting Features

### Multiple Fasting Protocols Supported
The app supports every major intermittent fasting approach:
- **12:12** — the beginner-friendly baseline
- **16:8** — the most popular protocol
- **18:6, 20:4** — advanced time-restricted eating
- **OMAD** — one meal a day
- **Extended fasts** — up to 72 hours with detailed stage tracking

### Science-Based Fasting Stages
This is what sets Fasting and Walking apart. As your fast progresses, the app explains exactly what's happening in your body at each stage:

| Stage | What's Happening |
|-------|-----------------|
| Early fasting | Blood sugar normalizing, insulin dropping |
| Glycogen depletion | Body switches to fat stores |
| Fat burning | Free fatty acids rising |
| Ketone production | Brain begins running on ketones |
| Light ketosis | Mental clarity, reduced hunger |
| Deep ketosis | Peak fat adaptation |
| Autophagy | Cellular cleanup begins |
| Extended metabolic | Deep repair processes activated |

Understanding *why* fasting works makes it easier to stick with. Instead of just watching a timer, you understand what your body is doing — and that keeps you motivated.

### Hydration Guidance
Staying hydrated during a fast is critical. The app includes built-in hydration reminders and guidance so you fast safely and effectively.

## Walking & Step Tracking

### Daily Step Goals with Progress Rings
Track your daily steps with visual progress rings that fill up as you walk. At a glance you can see how close you are to your daily goal — motivating you to take that extra walk at the end of the day.

### No Extra Hardware Needed
The app uses your iPhone's built-in motion sensors — no wearable required. If you have an Apple Watch, it syncs seamlessly too.

## Why Fasting + Walking Work Together

Research consistently shows that combining intermittent fasting with regular low-intensity exercise accelerates fat loss compared to either approach alone. Walking during a fasted state particularly enhances fat oxidation — your body burns more stored fat when you walk while fasted.

Fasting and Walking is built around this principle: track both in one app, understand the science, and build a sustainable routine.

## App Design Philosophy

Fasting and Walking was deliberately designed to be minimal:
- **No account required** — open the app and start
- **No social feed** — no distractions, no comparisons
- **Clean visuals** — easy to read at a glance
- **No data collection** — your health data stays on your device

Health apps should help you, not overwhelm you. This one does exactly what it says.
