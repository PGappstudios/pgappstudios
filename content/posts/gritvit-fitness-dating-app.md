# GritVit: The Fitness Dating App That Matches Athletes by Workout Data

What if your next match was based on how hard you train — not just how good your photos are? That's the idea behind **GritVit**, the fitness dating app that matches serious athletes using verified workout data.

GritVit reads your real workout history from **Apple Health** and **Google Health Connect** and calculates a **Fitness Score** that reflects how consistently and seriously you train. No filters, no fake bios — just proof of work.

## What Is GritVit?

GritVit is a dating app built specifically for people who live an active lifestyle. It connects runners, gym regulars, CrossFit athletes, cyclists, and anyone who takes their health seriously — and it verifies that dedication with data.

Instead of swiping based on photos alone, GritVit gives every user a **Trust Level** (L1–L5) and a **Fitness Score** (0–100) calculated from real training metrics pulled directly from your wearable device or phone.

## How GritVit Works

### Your Fitness Score (0–100)
GritVit calculates your score from eight real training signals:

| Signal | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Resting Heart Rate | 20% |
| Workout Frequency | 18% |
| Cardio Volume | 12% |
| Recency of Training | 10% |
| Daily Steps | 10% |
| Active Calories | 8% |
| Longest Session | 6% |
| Daily Distance | 6% |

Your score tells the whole story of your fitness lifestyle — not just what you look like.

### Trust Level System (L1–L5)
GritVit uses a **Trust Level** to show how verifiable your fitness data is:
- **L1–L4**: Based on accumulated workout history
- **L5 (Elite)**: Requires 30 consecutive days of wearable data with zero breaks

The higher your Trust Level, the more credibility you carry in the community.

### Privacy First
Your raw workout data **never leaves your device**. GritVit only uploads aggregated scores — so your personal health records stay private while still proving your fitness level.
