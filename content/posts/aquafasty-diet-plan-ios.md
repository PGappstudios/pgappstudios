Most health apps do one thing. **Aquafasty** does three — and does them all together, in one clean interface. Track your water intake, daily steps, and intermittent fasting simultaneously to build the complete foundation of a healthy lifestyle.

## What Is Aquafasty?

Aquafasty is a health and diet planning app for iPhone that combines three evidence-based pillars of healthy living:

1. **Hydration tracking** — water is the most important and most neglected health habit
2. **Step tracking** — daily movement is the most accessible form of exercise
3. **Intermittent fasting** — one of the most researched approaches to weight management

Instead of juggling three separate apps, Aquafasty brings all three into one cohesive daily routine — so you always know where you stand on each habit.

## Why These Three Habits Together?

Research shows that hydration, movement, and meal timing interact synergistically:
- **Water** supports metabolism, energy levels, and reduces false hunger signals
- **Steps** burn calories, improve cardiovascular health, and support mental wellbeing
- **Fasting** regulates insulin, promotes fat burning, and simplifies eating decisions

Tracking all three together gives you a complete picture of your health habits — and makes it harder to excuse a bad day on just one metric.

## Key Features

### Hydration Tracking
Log your water intake throughout the day and track progress toward your daily goal. Aquafasty sends reminders to drink water so you stay consistently hydrated — especially important during fasting windows.

### Step Tracking
Your daily step count is one of the most reliable health indicators. Aquafasty tracks your steps automatically and shows your progress toward your goal with clean visual indicators.

### Fasting Timer & Support
Start a fasting timer, track your current fasting window, and get guidance on what your body is doing during different stages of a fast. Multiple fasting protocols supported.

### Clean, Distraction-Free Design
No clutter. No unnecessary features. Aquafasty is designed to be fast to check and easy to use every day — because a health app you actually use is the only kind that works.
