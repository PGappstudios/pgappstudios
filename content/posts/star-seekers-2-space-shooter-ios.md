Pilot your starship through waves of alien enemies with responsive, smooth controls. The top-down perspective gives you full visibility of the battlefield — dodge incoming fire, line up your shots, and unleash your weapons on everything in your path.

Collect credits and unlock a roster of ships, each with unique power variations and playstyles. Upgrade your weapons and abilities to become increasingly formidable as you push into harder galaxies.

The upgrade system rewards persistence — the deeper you go, the stronger you become.

### Multiple Galaxy Levels
Journey through a series of distinct galaxies, each with increasing enemy complexity and new environmental challenges. New enemy types, new formations, and new surprises await at every level.

No internet connection required. Star Seekers 2 is fully offline-capable — perfect for commutes, flights, or anywhere you want a great game without needing Wi-Fi.
