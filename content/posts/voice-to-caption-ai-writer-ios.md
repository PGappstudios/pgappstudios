## What Is Voice To Caption?

Voice To Caption is an iOS app that uses artificial intelligence to convert your voice recordings into formatted social media captions. Instead of staring at a blank text field trying to write something clever, you speak naturally — the app transcribes, refines, and formats your words into a ready-to-post caption complete with relevant hashtags.

It's built for content creators, social media managers, entrepreneurs, and anyone who posts regularly and wants to save time.

## How It Works

1. **Speak** — Record yourself talking about your post, idea, or topic
2. **AI Writes** — The app transcribes your voice and uses AI to craft a polished caption
3. **Hashtags Generated** — Smart hashtags are automatically added based on your content
4. **Copy & Post** — One tap to copy your caption and paste it into any app

It takes about 30 seconds from speaking to having a caption ready to post.
