# DinkUp: The All-in-One Pickleball App for Courts, Games & Tournaments

Pickleball is the fastest-growing sport in the world — and finding games, courts, and players just got a whole lot easier. **DinkUp** is the iOS app built specifically for the pickleball community, putting courts, games, tournaments, and players all in one place.

Whether you're a beginner looking for your first open game or a competitive player chasing a city leaderboard spot, DinkUp has everything you need.

## What Is DinkUp?

DinkUp is a free pickleball app for iOS that centralizes your entire pickleball experience. Forget scrolling through Facebook groups or texting your local club — DinkUp gives you real-time court data, one-tap game joining, and professional tournament management right from your phone.

## Key Features of DinkUp

### Find Courts Near You
DinkUp's live map (powered by Google Maps) shows every pickleball, tennis, and padel court near you. You can see real-time crowd levels and which players are currently on the court — so you never show up to a packed or empty court again.

### Join Open Games Instantly
Browse open games in your area complete with court details, skill levels, and player availability. Join any game with a single tap — no phone calls, no back-and-forth. You can also create your own game and invite the community in seconds.

### Host & Play in Tournaments
DinkUp makes tournament management effortless:
- **Single Elimination** and **Round Robin** formats
- Automatic bracket generation with bye handling
- Live leaderboards tracking wins, losses, points for, and point differential
- Auto-advancing brackets
- In-app notifications for all participants when matches conclude

No more spreadsheets. No more manual bracket updates. DinkUp handles it all automatically.

### ELO Ranking & City Leaderboards
After every match, your rank updates automatically. DinkUp uses an **ELO-style ranking system** with live city leaderboards so you always know where you stand against other players in your area. The app also integrates **DUPR ratings** for players who use the standard pickleball rating system.

### Connect with Your Pickleball Community
- Direct messaging between players
- Community feed for sharing highlights and court discoveries
- Group creation for regular crews and clubs
- Browse player profiles filtered by skill level
- **"Dinks" reputation scoring** — your community credibility score
