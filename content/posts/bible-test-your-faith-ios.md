Whether you've been a believer for decades or are just beginning to explore your faith, this app meets you where you are.

## What Is Bible: Test Your Faith?

Bible: Test Your Faith is a free iPhone app that combines Bible trivia, scripture memorization, and daily verse reflection into a gentle, accessible quiz experience. It's not about competitive scoring — it's about growing your knowledge of God's Word one question at a time.

The app is designed to be encouraging, not intimidating. Every question is an opportunity to learn, revisit familiar passages, and discover new ones.

## Features

### Daily Bible Questions
Start each day with a Bible question that prompts reflection and learning. Questions span both the Old and New Testament, covering stories, prophecies, letters, and teachings.

### Inspiring Verses
Alongside quizzes, the app surfaces inspiring scripture verses to encourage and uplift you throughout the day.

### All-Ages Quiz Experience
The app is designed for **all ages** — from children learning Bible stories for the first time to lifelong believers revisiting familiar scripture. Questions are accessible without being too easy for experienced readers.

### Topics Covered
- Old Testament stories and history
- New Testament teachings and parables
- The life of Jesus
- The Epistles and letters of Paul
- The Psalms and Proverbs
- Prophets and prophecy
- The Book of Revelation

## Why Use an App for Bible Study?

Your phone is already in your hand dozens of times a day. Bible: Test Your Faith turns those moments into brief, meaningful encounters with scripture. Five minutes of daily Bible questions adds up — over weeks and months, your knowledge and confidence in the Word grows significantly.

It's not a replacement for reading the Bible — it's a companion to it.
